About

I have been a registered nurse for 35 years and am committed to integrating my nursing education with a holistic integrative model of healing. This decision changed my life and allowed me to heal at a very deep level. It is my vision to use the skills and specific mind/body methods taught in this four year healing science school to assist others in their healing process as well.

I have a private healing practice in Reno, Nevada and am available for consultations and healing sessions in person, by phone, or by Skype.
